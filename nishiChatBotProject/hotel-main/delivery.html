Delivery-policy

  • Thank you for choosing our food delivery website. This delivery policy describes our delivery process and policies.
  • Delivery process
  • Once you place an order on our website, we will process it and provide you with an estimated delivery time. We will then assign a delivery partner to deliver your order to the delivery address you provided. You will receive a notification when your order is on its way and when it has been delivered.
  • Delivery fees
  • We may charge a delivery fee for each order, which will be displayed on the checkout page before you confirm your order. The delivery fee may vary depending on your location and the size of your order.
  • Delivery areas
  • We have specific delivery areas, and we will only deliver to addresses within these areas. You can check if your address is within our delivery areas by entering your address on the checkout page. If your address is not within our delivery areas, we may not be able to deliver to you.
  • Delivery times
  • We will do our best to deliver your order within the estimated delivery time. However, delivery times may vary depending on various factors, such as traffic and weather conditions. We do not guarantee delivery within the estimated delivery time, and we are not liable for any delay in delivery.
  • Delivery instructions
  • You can provide specific delivery instructions on the checkout page, such as gate codes or building access information. We will pass these instructions to our delivery partner to ensure smooth delivery. However, we are not responsible for any delay or failed delivery due to incorrect or incomplete delivery instructions.
